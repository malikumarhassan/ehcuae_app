EHC Pioneering a Sustainable Future

The UAE has long been a global leader in energy innovation, balancing its status as a major oil producer with its commitment to renewable energy and sustainability. EHC’s investment strategy is deeply aligned with the UAE’s vision to diversify its energy sources, reduce carbon emissions, and embrace cleaner alternatives.

UAE Energy Milestones

The UAE’s Energy Strategy 2050 targets increasing the share of clean energy in the total energy mix to 50%. Clean gas alternatives are pivotal to achieving this goal. EHC’s subsidiaries, especially Al Fanar Gas, play a vital role in supporting this vision.

Waste Gas to Energy Economy

The growing energy demand driven by the expansion of data centers and AI technologies presents both challenges and opportunities. Waste and untapped gases, such as flared gas and methane from landfills or stranded gases or even natural gases, can provide a significant and underutilized energy source. By capturing and converting these gases into usable energy, EHC aims to leverage its expertise to oversee opportunities in such demand, while supporting key sectors in UAE and the region to reducing their environmental impacts

In EHC and collaboration with Holders Investment, NexGen is leading the way in harnessing this potential. The company specializes in converting waste gases into electricity, which can power digital infrastructure like data centers, crypto mining facilities and AI operations. This approach not only addresses energy needs but also supports the UAE's sustainability goals by reducing carbon emissions.

Always ready to push the boundaries, especially when it comes to Through its innovative solutions, NexGen will play a crucial role in meeting the vision of the EHC while contributing to the company value and growth.

Digitizing Energy Infrastructure

EHC’s technology arm, ISI, is integrating IoT, artificial intelligence (AI) into energy operations. AI-driven predictive maintenance and smart energy grids and metering are just a few examples of how ISI is revolutionizing energy efficiency and reliability in the UAE.

Looking Ahead

EHC is dedicated to leading the UAE’s energy transition by leveraging its expertise and investments to create a more sustainable and resilient energy future. We warmly invite you to connect with us and be part of this journey.
